<Section icon={Shield} title="Advanced Team Building and Combat Meta">

Once you have secured a few of the **RNG Heroes best heroes**, you need to optimize your team layout. RNG Heroes allows you to unlock multiple "Equip Slots." A team of five average heroes is often better than a team with one "God-tier" hero and four empty slots because of the way total DPS is calculated.

Combat is automated, but your preparation is manual. Focus on these three pillars of the combat meta:

1.  **Slot Expansion**: Every new slot you buy adds 100% of a hero's damage to your total. This is the most consistent way to increase power.
2.  **Rarity Synergy**: Some higher-rarity heroes have hidden multipliers that aren't immediately obvious from their base level.
3.  **Size Matters**: Upgrading your hero size isn't just cosmetic; it often correlates with a larger hit box, allowing your heroes to engage monsters from a slightly further distance.

| Team Strategy | Best For | Requirement |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **The Carry** | Early Game | 1 High-Tier Hero (Assassin) |
| **The Swarm** | Mid Game | 5+ Slots with B-Tier Heroes |
| **The Elite** | End Game | 5+ Slots with A/S-Tier Rolls |

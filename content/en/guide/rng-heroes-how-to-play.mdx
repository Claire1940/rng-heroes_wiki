<Section icon={Sparkles} title="Mastering Rolling Odds and Luck Upgrades">

The heart of **rng heroes how to play** is the rolling system. Every time you roll, the game calculates your result based on a set of rarity odds. While the Warrior is common, legendary and mythic heroes have odds reaching into the millions or even trillions. To succeed, you must systematically improve your **Luck** stat using the Gold earned from monster battles.

Luck acts as a multiplier that shifts the probability curve in your favor. However, a common mistake for new players is spending all their Gold on Luck while neglecting combat damage. If your heroes take too long to defeat monsters, your Gold income will stall, preventing further Luck upgrades.

| Upgrade Type | Primary Effect | Cost Scaling | Recommended Timing |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Luck** | Improves rarity of hero rolls | Exponential | Buy when combat is fast |
| **Combat Damage** | Increases hero DPS | Linear | Buy if enemies take >5s to die |
| **Gold Drop** | Increases Gold per kill | Linear | Buy after first zone unlock |
| **XP Drop** | Increases level progression | Moderate | Buy when leveling feels slow |

<Callout type="warning" title="Efficiency Alert">
Do not perform long rolling sessions if your Luck is still at base levels. It is much more efficient to farm Gold for 10 minutes, buy three Luck upgrades, and then roll, rather than rolling 100 times with no bonuses.
</Callout>

</Section>

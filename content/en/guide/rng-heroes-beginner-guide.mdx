<Section icon={Shield} title="Advanced Tips for Faster Growth">

Once you have the basics down, you can optimize your gameplay to reach the "Final Available Area" faster than the average player. The key is balancing your active playtime with smart resource management.

- **Auto-Roll Strategy**: Set your auto-roll filters to automatically delete "Common" heroes like the Warrior once your team is full. This prevents your inventory from clogging up.
- **AFK Farming**: If the game allows, find a spot where monsters respawn quickly and leave your team to farm. Ensure you have upgraded your **Gold Drop** stat to make this time more valuable.
- **Luck Stacking**: Combine the Group Bonus (+1 Luck) with permanent Luck upgrades. As you progress, look for limited-time events or new codes that might offer temporary Luck potions.
- **Scaling Up**: As your Gold income increases, the cost of upgrades will also rise. Always calculate the "Return on Investment." If a Luck upgrade costs 50,000 Gold but an Area Unlock costs 40,000, the Area Unlock is usually better as it provides access to higher-paying monsters.

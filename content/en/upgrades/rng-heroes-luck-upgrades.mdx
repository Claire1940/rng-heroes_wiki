<Section icon={Swords} title="Farming Gold for Upgrades">

To fund your **rng heroes luck upgrades**, you must optimize your Gold per Minute (GPM). Gold is dropped by every monster defeated, but the amount scales based on the monster's health and the zone's difficulty. 

| Zone Name | Monster Type | Avg Gold Drop | Difficulty |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Starter Area** | Slimes / Goblins | 5 - 15 | Very Easy |
| **Forest Biome** | Wolves / Spiders | 40 - 100 | Easy |
| **Desert Zone** | Scorpions / Mummies | 250 - 600 | Moderate |
| **Ice Tundra** | Yetis / Frost Giants | 1,200 - 3,500 | Hard |
| **Volcanic Peak** | Fire Dragons / Golems | 10,000+ | Very Hard |

**Key Farming Tactics:**
- **Slot Priority**: More heroes mean more simultaneous attacks. Always buy a new Team Slot if you have a hero in your inventory that is stronger than 50% of your current active team's average power.
- **Overkill Penalty**: If you deal 1,000 damage to a monster with 10 HP, you are wasting potential GPM. Move to a harder zone where your damage is utilized more efficiently.
- **Gold Drop Upgrades**: While Luck is the goal, investing in the Gold Drop upgrade pays for itself within approximately 20 minutes of active farming.

<Callout type="tip" title="Efficiency Check">
If you can one-shot every enemy in your current zone, you are farming too low. If it takes more than 15 hits to kill one enemy, you are farming too high. Find the "sweet spot" where enemies die in 3-5 hits for maximum GPM.
</Callout>
